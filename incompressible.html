Incompressible Liquid Constructors

The Water Library provides the following component constructors:

  • WaterSource() - Creates a water source component.
  • WaterPipe() - Creates a water pipe component.
  • WaterTank() - Creates a water tank component.

Common Inputs

The component constructors share common inputs:

  • capacity - Specifies the capacity of the Incompressible Liquid in liters.
  • material - Defines the material of the component (e.g., plastic, metal).

WaterSource

The WaterSource component constructor has additional inputs:

  • flowRate - Sets the water flow rate in liters per minute.
  • temperature - Specifies the water temperature in degrees Celsius.
